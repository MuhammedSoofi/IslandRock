About Us

Island Rock your premier destination for all your mobile phone spare parts needs. Established in 2018, we have been serving customers in Dubai, UAE, with a commitment to excellence and quality service.

At Island Rock, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of mobile phone spare parts, catering to various makes and models. Whether you're in need of a screen replacement, battery, charger, or any other spare part, we've got you covered.

Our dedication to customer satisfaction is reflected in the quality of our products and the reliability of our service. We understand the importance of your mobile device and strive to ensure that you have access to the parts you need to keep your devices running smoothly.

Conveniently located in Dubai, UAE, Island Rock is your one-stop shop for all your mobile phone spare parts requirements. Our knowledgeable staff is here to assist you in finding the right parts for your device and addressing any questions or concerns you may have
